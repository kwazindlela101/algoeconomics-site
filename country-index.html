What the ACI Does

The ACI takes thousands of fragmented signals — speeches, policies, protests, court rulings, corruption cases, regulatory shifts, geopolitical events — and compresses them into one clean, continuously updated score. At a glance, you see how stable, investable, and well-governed a country is right now. And whether it's improving or deteriorating.

No anecdotes. No insider networks. No annual PDFs. Just evidence, updated as the world moves.

Why It Matters

Decision-makers today are forced to rely on outdated ratings, intuition, and personal networks. The ACI removes that guesswork. It levels the playing field by making governance signals transparent and accessible to anyone — not just insiders. It shows momentum, not snapshots: not where a country was last year, but where it's headed. It rewards real improvements that often go unnoticed, and flags risks early.

This is the first system that treats governance as a real-time, quantifiable variable. It fills the last blind spot in global investment intelligence.
